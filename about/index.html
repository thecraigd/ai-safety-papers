About the Project

The Guardrail is an independent research infrastructure project aimed at making AI safety research more accessible. It was created by Craig Dickson, an independent researcher focused on AI safety tooling and community infrastructure.

This project is made possible by a BlueDot Impact Rapid Grant, which supports initiatives that help researchers and practitioners engage with AI safety.
